Announcements

Cubstart is a 2-unit full-stack/mobile development course for beginner developers. Whether you need relevant project experiences to kickstart your resume, or if you have a burning desire to build but just don’t know where to start, Cubstart is the course for you. We provide unique weekly guided full-stack projects, mentorship, and technical workshops. The latter half of the semester will be dedicated to applying your skills to develop a viable product/application with a team and a mentor. We encourage those with little or no CS experience to join! If you are someone who’s passionate about learning and wants to create unique projects from start to finish, Cubstart is the place for you!
